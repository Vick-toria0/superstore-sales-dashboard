# Key Insight
While the West region led in both total sales ($739,814) and profit ($110,799), 
a closer look revealed that the South region outperformed Central on profit 
despite lower sales volume ($46,749 vs $39,865 profit, with Central actually 
having higher sales). This suggests Central may have efficiency issues — such 
as heavier discounting or higher costs — worth investigating further.
